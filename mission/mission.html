Elder Clark Shaeffer

Mexico Aguascalientes Mission

I absolutely loved my time as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Some say that missionaries go to baptize other people, to learn about God or the Book of Mormon, or other things; I know now that I went to testify of Jesus Christ. He was and is the Savior of the world, and I was given two years to talk about it. Miracles ensued.

This isn't an obituary! I just want to remind myself of the happiness I felt as a missionary. Life has been difficult. It's only gotten harder since I've been home. So I need to remember.

Companions, converts, callings; walking, talking, teaching; love and hope and patience. Mexico is my home, and I will return to live there again soon.
